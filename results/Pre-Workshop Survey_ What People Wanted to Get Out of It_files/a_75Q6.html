Pre-Workshop Survey: What People Wanted to Get Out of It

Stata Translation Workshop · Pre-Workshop Survey

What people wanted to get out of the workshop

Drawn from "Is there anything specific you want to get out of today's workshop?" — 8 of 13 respondents gave a substantive answer (5 left it blank or wrote "/"). Same packed-circle style as the post-workshop cloud: right angles only, tight regular spacing, size and color track how concrete/shared each goal was.

Bigger & more colorful = a stronger, more specific or more shared goal · small, gray & faint = general topic, not a goal in itself

Dominant goal: converting their own Stata code or package to R — 3 of 8 respondents said this directly, and it's the only goal more than one person shared. Everything else was a distinct, individually-held goal: developing/generalizing a translation workflow, judging the plugin's strengths and weaknesses, understanding how skills/plugins are built, learning to evaluate Claude's output before trusting it, stress-testing translation on more complex code, and just exploring what Claude can do. With only 8 answers, read this as a spread of individual motivations rather than a consensus beyond the Stata→R goal.
