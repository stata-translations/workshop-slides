Blue = fluent in one tends to mean less fluent in the other. Red = fluency in both moves together. Diagonal (1.00) is a language against itself and carries no information.

Strongest relationship: Stata ↔ Python, ρ = −0.85 (n=11) — the more fluent someone is in Stata, the less fluent they tend to be in Python, and vice versa. Stata ↔ R is also negative (ρ = −0.59, n=13). R, Python and Matlab correlate weakly positively with each other (ρ = 0.04–0.38).

Two respondents left Python and Matlab blank (treated as missing, not "No"), so those pairs are based on n=11 rather than n=13. With samples this small, treat every value here as a rough, exploratory signal — not a robust statistical finding.
